## Google Business Profile

This is the most important thing you can do to rank locally on Google.

**If you haven't already:**
1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Search for your business name
3. Claim it (Google will send a postcard with a verification code)
4. Fill out every field completely: hours, services, photos, description

**After claiming:**
- Add at least 10 photos (inside, outside, team, work examples)
- Post updates at least once a month
- Respond to every review (positive and negative)

1. Search for your business on Google
2. Click "Get more reviews" in your Business Profile panel
3. Copy the short link and share it via text/email

## Local Citations — Submit Your Business to These Directories

**What are citations?** A "citation" is any mention of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) on the internet. Google uses citation consistency as a local ranking factor. The more places your exact NAP appears, the more Google trusts your business is real and located where you say it is.

**Critical: Use your business name, address, and phone number exactly the same on every directory — no abbreviations, no variations.**
